Biryani Recipe

Biryani is flavoured and aromatic rice dash, popular in Pakistan and India.This recipe will guide you to make the best biryani for your family and friends.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of basmati rice
  • 1 kilo chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion,sliced
  • 4 tomatos, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp tumeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves for garnish

Insructions:

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for about 30 minutes
  2. Heat oil in a large pot and saute the sliced onions until golden brown
  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and saute for 1 minute
  4. Mix the chicken pieces and cooked until they are lightly browned
  5. Add the chopped tomatos, yogurt, and spices.Cook until the mixture thicken
  6. Boil 4 cups of water in aseparate pot.Add the soaked rice and cook until 70% done
  7. Layer the rice and chicken mixture in the pot.Garnish with coriander and mint leaves
  8. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes to let the flavors meld togather
  9. Serve hot with raitha and salaad
