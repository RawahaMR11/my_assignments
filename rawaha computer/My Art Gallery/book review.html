Short History Of Jawab-e-shikwa:


Allama Muhammad Iqbal wrote the Jawab-e-shikwa in reponse of Shikwa.When he erote the Shikwa, peoples stated opposing him. In this he answered that questions that he did in the Shikwa.

Summary:

"Jawab-e-shikwa" by "Allama Muhammad Iqbal" is a powerful response to his earlier poem "Shikwa", where he Questioned God about the struggles of Muslim Ummah. In this aequel, Iqbal imagines God's reply,addressing the complaint's with wisdoms and divining reasoning. The poem highlights the decline of Muslims due to their abundonment of the true faith, unity, and moral values.
